# 📊 Revenue Drivers & Profitability Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes key factors affecting revenue and profitability, including product categories, customer segments, regional performance, and discount impact.

The goal is to identify the main revenue drivers and provide actionable business recommendations.

---

## 📁 Dataset
- Total Customers: 793  
- Revenue: $31.8M  
- Profit: $14.8M  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. Product Categories
- **Office Supplies** generates **83% of total revenue**
→ Main driver of business performance

---

### 2. Regional Performance
- **West region** leads in revenue
→ Requires further margin analysis to evaluate efficiency

---

### 3. Customer Segmentation
- VIP customers represent **58% of customer base**
- Generate **93% of total revenue**

👉 High dependency on a single customer segment

---

### 4. Discount Impact
- Discounts up to **20%** maintain strong profitability  
- Discounts above **20–30% significantly reduce profit**

👉 Over-discounting negatively impacts business performance

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus on **VIP customer retention**  
- Optimize discount strategy (avoid high discounts)  
- Strengthen diversification across customer segments  
- Analyze profitability by region (not only revenue)
